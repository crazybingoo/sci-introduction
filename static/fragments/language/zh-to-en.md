# Language Fragment: Chinese Or Mixed Notes To English

Use this fragment when the user's source material is Chinese or mixed Chinese-English.

- Reason about structure in Chinese when helpful.
- Produce manuscript prose in English unless the user asks otherwise.
- Convert Chinese value claims into evidence-bounded English claims.
- Do not translate Chinese notes literally when they create boilerplate or overclaiming.
- Preserve author intent but ask when the English claim boundary is unclear.
